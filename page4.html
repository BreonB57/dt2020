To start off, the white train sets the motor to 100%





After a delay of 6 seconds, the red train sets its motor to 100%





When any train passes Trip A, the barriers go down, and the red lights flash




As soon as the train passes Trip C, a delay starts, and once completed, the barrier goes back up, and the lights stop flashing



The red train will do the exact same


If both triggers happen to be on at the same time, the barrier will not go up, and will stay down awaiting the next train.

The lights are able to flicker, by following a script that turns them off, adds a delay, then turns them back on, then adds another delay, etc.
